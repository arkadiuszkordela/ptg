Python Text Games (PTG) is a response to the mainstream of video games. The idea is not based on negating

the direction in which electronic entertainment is heading. This is just an alternative path of development that aims

to push the world of electronic entertainment in the opposite direction. We appreciate the skills of developers creating

VR games and we are aware of the possibilities of this technology and all the benefits resulting from it. We do not mind

raising funds by selling these games because we know that money is needed not only for progress but also for fair

remuneration of those for whom game development is a job. We respect the privacy of large companies and the fact that

they do not make their code publicly available. Nevertheless, we do something completely different...



1. Our games are based on simple code. They are devoid of graphics and are based only on a written story.

2. Our games do not absorb the player's imagination, but are designed to stimulate it.

3. Our games are focused on an interesting story, not on gameplay.

4. Our games do not require expensive equipment to play them.

5. Our games are made by passionate amateurs.

6. Our games are completely free.

7. Our games are open. All their code is publicly available.



We are creating a community that wants to develop this project together and make new friends. Feel invited!
